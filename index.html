danielkon / share.js — demo page

1. Installation:

download share.js

OR grab link from GitHub: 

<script src="https://danielkon.github.io/share.js/share.js"></script>

2. Quick Usage:

<button onclick="share('fb');">Facebook</button>
<button onclick="share('tw');">Twitter</button>
<button onclick="share('vk');">VK</button>
<button onclick="share('tg');">Telegram</button>
<button onclick="share();">Share</button>
